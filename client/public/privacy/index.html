Privacy Policy

Ephemeral Drops · Last updated 30 June 2026

End-to-end encrypted · No accounts · No tracking

Ephemeral Drops lets you create encrypted, self-destructing "drops" — text, images, audio, or files — that are gated to specific recipients and disappear after viewing or expiry. This policy explains exactly what the service can and cannot see. It applies to the website and to the desktop and Android apps.

The short version

Your content is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves it. The server only ever stores ciphertext (scrambled data) and never sees your message text, files, or the real usernames of recipients. There are no accounts, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking.

What we cannot see

  • The contents of your drops — they are encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device.
  • The decryption keys — the master key is wrapped for each recipient using a key derived from their username and is never sent to us in usable form.
  • Recipient usernames in plaintext — only a SHA-256 hash of each username is stored.

What is stored

To deliver a drop, the service stores the minimum necessary, all of which is deleted when the drop expires or is fully claimed:

  • The encrypted payload (ciphertext) of your drop.
  • Wrapped (encrypted) keys and SHA-256-hashed recipient usernames.
  • Minimal metadata: the drop's random ID, a verbal code, time-to-live, and an optional plaintext hint you choose to add.

Data retention

Drops are ephemeral. Every drop is automatically and permanently deleted when its time-to-live expires (from 5 minutes up to 24 hours), or immediately after all recipients have opened a "view-once" drop. Encrypted blobs are removed from storage at the same time. We do not keep backups of expired drops.

Network and security

  • All connections use HTTPS.
  • Your IP address may be processed transiently to apply rate limits and prevent abuse; it is not stored alongside your drops or used to profile you.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking and we do not sell or share data with advertisers.

Service providers

The app runs on hosting infrastructure and uses Cloudflare R2 object storage to hold encrypted payloads. These providers only ever receive ciphertext and process data on our behalf to operate the service. They cannot read your drop contents.

Permissions on mobile

The Android app requests internet access to reach the service. It accesses files, the camera, or the share sheet only when you actively choose to attach or save content. It does not collect contacts, location, or device identifiers.

Children

Ephemeral Drops is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
